Our Story

Pure Roots began as a small family venture in 1998, rooted in a deep love for nature and a passion for gardening. Founders Emma and John Greenway, avid gardeners themselves, started selling their homegrown plants and flowers at local farmer's markets. Their commitment to quality and their personal touch quickly garnered a loyal following. What started as a modest stall blossomed into a full-fledged nursery, and Pure Roots was born.

Over the years, Pure Roots has grown into a thriving business, but our core values remain unchanged. We are still family-owned and operated, with the next generation of Greenways, Emma and John's children, now deeply involved in the business. Our journey has been one of growth, learning, and deepening our connection with nature and our community.

We invite you to visit Pure Roots and experience the beauty and serenity of our nursery for yourself. Wander through our lush gardens, discover new plant varieties, and chat with our friendly staff. Whether you're looking to start a new garden, find the perfect houseplant, or simply enjoy the natural beauty, Pure Roots is here for you.

Thank you for being a part of our story. We look forward to growing with you!
